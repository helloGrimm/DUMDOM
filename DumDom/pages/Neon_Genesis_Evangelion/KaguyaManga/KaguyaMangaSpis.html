MANGA

Written and illustrated by Aka Akasaka. Kaguya-sama Love is War began in Shueisha's Miracle Jump magazine in May 2015, but got moved to Weekly Young Jump in March 2016. As of December 2022, there are 28 Japanese volumes published with more than 22 million copies in circulation, including spin-offs and digital sales. The manga officially ended with Chapter 281, which came out on November 2, 2022.
