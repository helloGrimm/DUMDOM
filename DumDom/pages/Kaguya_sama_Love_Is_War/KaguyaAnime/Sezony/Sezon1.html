Season 1

Kaguya-sama wa Kokurasetai: Tensai-tachi no Renai Zunousen (かぐや様は告らせたい～天才たちの恋愛頭脳戦～"Kaguya-sama: Love is War") is an anime adaptation of the manga series, originally announced on Friday, June 1, 2018 along with its twitter account and website launch. It premiered on Saturday, January 12, 2019 and was produced by A-1 Pictures. The early screening of first 2 episodes of the anime was on January 5, 2019. The anime aired on TokyoMX, Gunma TV, Tochigi TV, and BS11. Aniplex of America streamed the series on FunimationNow, Hulu, and Crunchyroll. The anime is also available on Netflix
